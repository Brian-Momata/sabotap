How to play

Each round, one of you is the Searcher, the other the Caller. Roles swap every round.

The Caller secretly picks a target number from the grid, then earns sabotage charges by solving quick puzzles — and spends them to mess with the Searcher's grid.

The Searcher must find and tap the target before the fuse fills.

Find it in time → Searcher takes the round. Fuse fills first → Caller takes it.
